HERB CATALOG

Rumex Plant

Rumex is mostly a perennial herb with sturdy roots, paniculate inflorescences, and triangular fruits that are enveloped in the enlarged inner perianth. Roots of Rumex, also called dock root, have been reported for its therapeutic capacity of bacterial infections, inflammatory, tumor and cardiovascular diseases.

Punas plant

Punas is an erect, many branched, aromatic, perennial herb, 10-40 cm tall. Stems are glandular tomentose or matted villous with viscid, vitreous hairs, sometimes merely glandular. Leaves are petiolate, dull grayish green on upper surface and pale on the lower surface. It is used medicinally and has economic value to the botanicas and herbal shops that sell it in the form of dried powders, cut plants, live plants and oil extracts
